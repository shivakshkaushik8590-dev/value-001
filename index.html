Got Questions?

Frequently Answered Queries

Everything you need to know about our visualizer sandbox, estimate invoicing, and premium physical swatches.

How does the AI Room Designer visualizer work?
Upload a JPEG/PNG picture of your space in the AI Studio dashboard. Our depth-mapping boundary engine identifies walls, windows, ceilings, and existing parameters. You can overlay styles, drop resizable elements, and trigger luxury finishes renders in under 24 hours.
Are the cost estimations and materials billing ledger accurate?
Yes. All price rates for Carrara marble, German oak, acoustic panels, and lighting fixtures are synchronized in real time with global luxury vendor indexes. Complex labor multipliers and taxes are pre-calculated to avoid hidden charges.
Can I request real-world material swatches for my project?
Absolutely. Sourcing high-end materials can be challenging online. Under our Material Library page, click "Quick Preview" on any stone or wood item and submit your address to get premium swatch packets delivered directly to your doorstep.
Can we integrate Valure's estimators into our company developer systems?
Yes. Under our Developer tier and Enterprise options, we provide standard API access keys and full Web3D engine packages, allowing developers to embed interactive room design ledger frameworks into internal platforms.

Estimated invoicing rates represent industry averages and do not represent legally binding contracts until validated by our certified concierge teams.
