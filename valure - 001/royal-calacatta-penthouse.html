Royal Calacatta Penthouse

Gurgaon, Haryana — Private Residence

Project Overview

The Royal Calacatta Penthouse is an ultra-luxury private residence designed to establish a new benchmark in contemporary stone architecture. Focused entirely around premium bookmatched Italian Calacatta Gold marble, this penthouse brings natural fluid patterns and light into every main room.

High-contrast gold and grey veins run continuously across the major spaces, including a double-height bookmatched feature wall in the main saloon, a custom kitchen waterfall island, and the master bathroom suite. A floating white marble staircase with clear glass panels acts as the architectural anchor of the design, beautifully illuminated by architectural LED bands.
